About Us

Ever since we joined Code Next in 2017, one of our biggest problems was that it was quite hard to meet other Code Next members. There would be multiple cohorts in the same building but not much time to speak with them. We feel that this opportunity has decreased the amount of collaboration. Between Code Next members which sucks because Code Next is full of intelligent and driven students, types you may not come across often. During an ideation session at Launch in 2020, I, Paul Gaddy thought of PrograMeet, the solution to one of our biggest problems which was further worsened due to the coronavirus.
